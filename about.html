Our Corporate Identity

Flying Tours and Travel is a registered tourism agency dedicated to showcasing the unmatched beauty of the Pearl of Africa. We bridge the gap between adventure and comfort, offering guided expeditions through Uganda’s most iconic landscapes, from the savannahs of Queen Elizabeth Park to the misty heights of the Rwenzori Mountains.

Our Vision: To become the leading gateway for international and local travelers exploring the biodiversity of Uganda, ensuring conservation-friendly tourism at every milestone.

Company Expertise

We pride ourselves on a deep-rooted knowledge of Ugandan geography and wildlife behavior.

Destinations We Cover

Our portfolio includes exclusive coverage of Murchison Falls, where the Nile forces its way through the rocks, the Budongo Forest chimpanzee habitats, and the serene islands of Lake Bunyonyi. At Flying Tours and Travel, we ensure every journey is a masterclass in African scenery and wildlife.
